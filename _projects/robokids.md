The Robokids Interactive Robotics Recreation Center is the first and only AI-based indoor entertainment center in Iran. Developed by the knowledge-based company Green Robotic Future Arsam, Robokids has three branches in Tehran. It offers interactive, robotic, and modular games designed for children aged 2 to 14 years. So far, more than 41,000 unique users have visited Robokids. The center features various AI-powered interactive games, including Sami and Arso robots, interactive drawing, interactive sand play, interactive trampolines, Robonus, interactive climbing walls, and smart carpets.
