The Robokids, Interactive Robotic Play Center, is the first and only AI-powered indoor play center in Iran with three branches. It offers interactive, robotic, and modular games designed for children aged 2 to 14 years. So far, more than 41,000 unique users have visited Robokids. The center features various AI-powered interactive games, including Sami and Arsoo robots, interactive drawing, interactive sand play, interactive trampolines, Robonus, interactive climbing walls, and smart carpets.
