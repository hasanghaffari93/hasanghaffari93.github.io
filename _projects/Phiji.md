##### **Company Description**
Phiji is an AI-powered smart game for **education** through **entertainment** and **interaction**.

###### **Education**:
* Phiji teaches letters, words, pronunciation, numbers, mathematics, and enhance a significant range of cognitive abilities.

###### **Interaction**:

* Phiji has a **robot character** powered by **AI**, featuring visual and auditory perception to interact with children.
* Phiji can see its surroundings through its eyes (front and rear cameras of smartphone). 
* Phiji is capable of receiving and analyzing a child's speech through its ears (microphones) and can communicate with the child using its speech (speakers).
* Phiji identifies the child and recognizes the child's emotions and facial expressions.
* Phiji can detect objects in the surrounding environment and identify the elements moved by the child in the games in real-time.

###### **Entertainment**:
* Phiji offers a wide range of diverse games for children.

##### **Phiji Components**:

###### **Application**:

Phiji can run on all smartphones and tablets supporting **Android** and **iOS** operating systems.


###### **Stand**:
Phiji features a unique stand invented, designed, and manufactured. Phiji's stand is used as a holder for mobile phones. This stand is designed so that the rear camera of the mobile phone is positioned in front of a mirror. Therefore, this mirror reflects the image below the stand to the phone's camera. At the same time, the phone's front camera can also capture the user's face.

##### **Games**

##### **Alphabet Game**
The child learns how to write letters, a wide range of words, and how to pronounce them. In this game, the child assembles physical alphabet tokens to form predetermined words. Phiji uses AI to recognize the letters and provide feedback to the child.


During the game, Phiji asks the child to pronounce each word. Phiji listens to the pronounced word and provides feedback based on whether the pronunciation is correct or incorrect.

##### **Number Game**

Phiji teaches the child numbers and mathematical operations. These operations include addition and subtraction, starting with simple tasks and progressing to more complex ones. The child arranges physical number tokens to perform the mathematical operations. and Phiji deploy AI to detect the numbers and provide feedback on the correctness of the results.

##### **Memory Game**

This game enhances the child’s memory skills. Various shapes are briefly shown to the child to remember. The shapes then disappear, and the child is asked to place physical tokens containing those shapes in front of Phiji. Phiji uses AI to recognize the shapes and provides feedback on their accuracy.

##### **Galaxy Game**

This game takes place in a galactic environment where the child uses a spaceship and the available lasers to target enemy creatures. The game progresses from easy to difficult stages. The child must quickly maneuver the spaceship token to advance through the game levels. Phiji, using artificial intelligence, recognizes the spaceship in real-time, and the child can see its position on the game screen. This game enhances the child's motor skills and prevents the common drawbacks associated with smartphone games.
