### Introduction:




Phiji consists of integrated hardware and software. The software includes a smart application that can run on all smartphones and tablets with both Android and iOS operating systems. The hardware also includes a unique body along with various physical parts. Phiji combines three elements: entertainment, interaction, and education. Children, using this application and the physical components they have, engage in entertaining games while simultaneously learning. Since Phiji has its own distinct personality and identity, children interact with Phiji while playing and learning.


## Identity and Personality of Phiji
Phiji possesses a unique identity and personality. What shapes Phiji's personality is a combination of two components: the hardware (physical) and the software (digital), such that without either of these two parts, Phiji's identity will not take shape. This personality is also evident in Phiji's name. The name " Phiji" is a combination of two parts, "phi" and "ji," which respectively represent the concepts of "physical" and "digital".


### **Hardware**:

#### **Stand**:
Phiji features a unique body invented, designed, and manufactured. Phiji's body includes a stand used as a holder for mobile phones. This stand is designed so that the rear camera of the mobile phone is positioned in front of a mirror. Therefore, this mirror reflects the image below the stand to the phone's camera. At the same time, the phone's front camera can also capture the user's face.

Other hardware components of Phiji include physical tokens identified by mobile phones. The tokens include Persian and English alphabets, Persian and English numbers, cartoon characters, and a spaceship (body image and tokens).



## Phiji Software Section:
Phiji in the software (digital) section has a program or application that can run on all smartphones and tablets supporting Android and iOS operating systems. This application contains various sections and diverse games developed for the entertainment, education, and interaction for children. One of the functions of the software section is to create a pre-designed user interface for effective communication with children. This user interface includes an environment in which a child can interact with Phiji and play with it.
Another function of this section is to set up and control inputs and outputs of mobile devices for interaction with users. Inputs include front and rear cameras, microphones, touch screens, and accelerometers. Outputs include sound playback and a display screen. Another function of the software section is to control and execute the processor used in the hardware. This processor receives information from inputs such as images, sounds, touch, and device acceleration. It then processes this information, including running algorithms and artificial intelligence models. Finally, it provides the necessary outputs to the user, including images on the display and sound through the speaker.


### 1. Entertainment:
Since Phiji offers a wide range of diverse games for children, the entertainment element is an important aspect of Phiji. This entertainment includes both physical and mental games.


### 2. Interactivity:
Phiji has a robot-like character with the help of artificial intelligence, featuring visual perception, auditory perception, and natural language processing to interact with children. Phiji can see its surroundings through its eyes (front and rear cameras). Phiji is capable of receiving and analyzing a child's speech through its microphones and can communicate with the child using its speech (speakers). In this regard, Phiji not only identifies the child but also recognizes the child's emotions and facial expressions. This character can detect objects in the surrounding environment and identify the elements moved by the child in the games in real-time.


### 3. Education:
One of Phiji's goals is to educate children through entertainment and interaction with them. Teaching letters, words, pronunciation, mathematics, and enhancing a significant range of cognitive abilities in children are educational objectives of Phiji.
