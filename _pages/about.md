I have over five years of experience in AI, with a focus on Computer Vision. During my career, I’ve developed, implemented and deployed a wide range of computer vision algorithms and models for applications like image classification, object detection, person re-identification, multi-object tracking, and face recognition.

A part of my work involved building data pipelines for tasks such as data collection, annotation, augmentation, and preprocessing — primarily working with large datasets — to either fine-tune models or train them from scratch. Additionally, I’ve optimized and fine-tuned computer vision models for real-time performance across various mobile platforms (iOS, Android) and edge devices.

What excites me the most is collaborating with diverse teams to bring AI and Computer Vision models into production, particularly in fast-paced startup environments. I'm passionate about continually learning in this rapidly evolving field 🙂!  Feel free to check out one of my selected projects in which AI models deployed in production — I'd love to hear your thoughts 😃!
